S: start engine
{}: magneto switch actuator

B: wheel brake
shift+B: parking brake
Ctrl+B: toggle speed brake

][: extend / retract flaps
Home/End: elevator trim down/up

G/shift+G: gear up/down
L: Nose/tail sheel steering...?

Ctrl-V: cockpit view
v/V: cycle views
Shift+P: toggle 2D panel
c: toogle 3D panel
x/X: zoom in/out
Ctrl+X: reset zoom

HUD:
H/Shift+H: HUD color/bright
